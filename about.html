About Us

Fitti's Pizza is the passion project of brothers who share a lifelong love for pizza. After honing their skills at the San Francisco Baking Institute and earning VPN certification, they've turned their dream into reality with a cozy, takeout-only Detroit-style pizza spot. Whether you're ordering online from your favorite delivery service or picking up a delicious pie on a Friday or Saturday night, you're in for a treat!
